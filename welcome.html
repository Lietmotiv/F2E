How it Works

Whether you are a power-user that wants all the bells and whistles or you just want to put it on auto-pilot and forget about it, FaxForward can accomodate whatever faxing needs you have. Below are the two main parts of our service. Let us know if you need anything else.

Send a Fax

Sending a fax is easy:

  • send an email to your recipient's fax number with faxforward.com as the domain. i.e. 9705551212@faxforward.com and it'll be converted into a fax automatically. You'll get a notice when it sends. or...
  • Just browse to your inbox and click "Send Fax". We can handle almost any file type you can throw at us.

Get a Fax

Two ways to check your received faxes:

  • Just give people your fax number <%= this.SC.AuthData.GetDisplayInboundPhone() %> and we'll email you when a fax arrives. Simple.
  • You can also check your incoming faxes at your Dashboard. From there you can forward it, download it or view and print it.
